Introduction

Features

Your Notes, Your Way

Get Started

Start Your Obsidian Journey

Begin your Obsidian journey by downloading the software compatible with your operating system. Once installed, create a new vault to house your notes. Explore the user interface, familiarizing yourself with the file explorer, note editor, and graph view.


To start capturing your thoughts, create a new note. Experiment with basic formatting and linking to establish a foundation for your knowledge base.


  • # Installation: Download Obsidian for your preferred platform (Windows, macOS, Linux).
  • # Basic Setup: Create your first vault, explore the interface, and start taking notes.
  • # Core Concepts: Understand the foundation of Obsidian: notes, folders, links, and backlinks.
Core Concepts

To effectively use Obsidian, grasp these fundamental concepts:

  • # Notes: The building blocks of your knowledge base. Each note is a single file with its content.
  • # Folders: Organize your notes into hierarchical folders for better navigation.
  • # Links: Connect related notes by creating internal links within your vault.
  • # Backlinks: See which other notes link to a specific note, helping you discover connections.
